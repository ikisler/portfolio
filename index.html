About 🌿

Isabeau wrote her first lines of HTML and JavaScript at the age of thirteen, and hasn't stopped since. While she's spent time doing other things, including completing a business degree and working in education, coding for the web has always been with her. Isabeau is a recent graduate of Udacity's Front-End Nano Degree program, where she sharpened and expanded her skills, finishing the nine-month program in only five months. Her entrepreneur spirit and interest in the world has led her to self-publish booklets, operate a personal blog, and run a successful eBay business.
